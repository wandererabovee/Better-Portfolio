---
title: Absurdism vs Nihilism
date: 2024-05-18
topics: ["Philosophy"]
---

Absurdism, existentialism, and nihilism are all concerned with meaning — or the lack of it — in human existence. 

While nihilism denies all meaning, and existentialism seeks to define it personally, absurdism accepts the conflict between our desire for meaning and the silent universe. It laughs at the struggle, and insists we live on.

In this post, we’ll explore how absurdism offers the healthiest perspective when facing the absurdity of life.
